Target Audience

Families and friends who want to be involved in their community are the targeted audience of this website.

Personas

John Doe

  • Occupation: BYU-Idaho Student, Freshman
  • Demographics and Education: 19 years old. Social Sciene Major.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: This is John's first time being away from home. Being away from his family in Oregon, John wants to feel more like home by meeting new people. He believes that being more involved in the BYU-I community will do just that.
  • Social:A shy individual, but is willing to get out of his comfort zone.
  • Technology:Has the necessities in order to get things done. John uses a Macbook and iPhone.

Kim Lee

  • Occupation: Mother of two sons
  • Demographics and Education:31 years old. Graduated at BYU Provo Majoring in Nursing. After graduating, Kim worked at Sutter Health as a Clinical Triage Nurse.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site:The pandemic is slowly becoming less serious and being more maintained in Utah. Kim wants her children to get more exercise and make new friends. She wants to look through any activities or events in her community that's best for her sons.
  • Social:An outgoing person. She does her best to keep up with her small group of friends and go out with them once in awhile.
  • Technology:Due to the COVID-19 Pandemic, she began to work at home for her job. Kim has a standard desktop and Macbook for work. She also uses her iPhone frequently.
