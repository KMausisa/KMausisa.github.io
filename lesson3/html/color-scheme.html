Color Scheme

Primary Color: #77685D (Dim Gray) is a natural color that looks visually pleasing

Secondary Color: #483D3F (Black Coffee) will contrast with Dim Gray, making important information stand out.

Accent Color 1: #F4EBD9 (Eggshell) is for the background. With these darker colors, the elements such as the navigation will stand out more.

Accent Color 2: #A39A92 (Spanish Grey) will be used for the borders along the navigation and main sections of the website.
